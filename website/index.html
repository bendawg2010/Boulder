How it works.

Three steps. No streaks. No shame.

⌨️

Pick what you’re focusing on

Code, write, read, design — or roll your own custom tag. Each kind of focus stamps a different texture into your Boulder.

🪨

Hit Focus. Watch it grow.

Pixel by pixel, in real time. The longer you focus, the bigger Boulder gets. Stop and Boulder pauses — it doesn’t shrink.

🏔️

After 6–12 months: a mountain.

Release it into the sea. It joins your Mountain Range — the panorama of every Boulder you’ve ever grown.

Before you download

Free + open source · MIT licensed. macOS will say “Boulder can’t be opened” the first time — Apple charges $99/year for a Developer ID and Boulder is free. Inspect the source on GitHub.

  1. Drag Boulder.app to Applications
  2. Open it once → macOS pops “can’t be opened” → hit Done
  3. System Settings → Privacy & Security → scroll → Open Anyway
  4. Reopen → confirm “Open” → Boulder lives in your menubar 🪨

You only do this once. Future updates open normally.
